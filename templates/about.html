Company Vision

ezTruck started its journey in 2020 to revolutionise the logistics and supply-chain sectors digitally. A contemporary technology-driven platform for both drivers and customers with transparency. Here at ezTruck, we offer all logistics and supply-chain solutions, such as truck transport, professional packers and movers for house or office moving, e-commerce, local logistics, business logistics, and many more.
ezTruck is bringing you a complete logistics solution at your fingertips. Getting business for local truck drivers and getting your goods transported at the right price is not an easy task.... ezTruck created a portal for these customers who need their goods transported and for truck drivers together. After successfully running in Odisha for the last two years, we are looking forward to connecting all over India. ezTruck only recruits drivers with verified IDs and a good record. And customers find the best price ever. Which makes us the best and most trusted brand in the industry. Therefore, ezTruck is looking forward to elevating the logistics system digitally and making it easy to book trucks for business and household purposes. Simultaneously improving the lives of truck drivers and providing them with income on a daily basis.
