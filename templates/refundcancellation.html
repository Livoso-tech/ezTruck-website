Refund & Cancellation Policy

Refund Policy:

Refunds for cancelled shipments or logistics services will be processed according to the following guidelines:

Full Refund: If a cancellation request is made within the stipulated cancellation period, the customer will be eligible for a full refund of the service charges.

Partial Refund: For late cancellations, ezTruck Logistics reserves the right to deduct the applicable cancellation fee (15% of the total service cost) from the total refund amount.

Refund Processing: Refunds will be processed within 5 business days from the date of the cancellation request. The actual time it takes for the refund to be reflected in the customer's account may vary based on the payment method and financial institution.

Cancellation Policy:

Customers of ezTruck Logistics Pvt. Ltd. can request to cancel their logistics services in accordance with the following guidelines:

Order Cancellation: Customers can cancel an order or shipment by providing written notice to ezTruck Logistics at least 24 hours before the scheduled pickup/delivery date.

Partial Shipments: If a customer wishes to cancel a portion of their shipment while retaining other items, they must notify ezTruck Logistics within 48 hours of the scheduled pickup/delivery date.

Late Cancellation: Cancellation requests received within 12 hours of the scheduled pickup/delivery time may incur a cancellation fee of 15% of the total service cost.

Exceptional Circumstances:

In cases where ezTruck Logistics fails to provide the agreed-upon logistics services due to reasons beyond its control (such as natural disasters, labor strikes, or government regulations), the company will make reasonable efforts to provide refunds or reschedule the services, as applicable.
